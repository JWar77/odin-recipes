Nonna's Lasagna

This is the description.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed

Steps

  1. Dutch oven, cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until well browned
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water
  3. Season with sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, 1 teaspoon salt, pepper, and 2 tablespoons parsley. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally
  4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water
  5. n a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt
