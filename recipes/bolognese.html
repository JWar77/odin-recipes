Bolognese Recipe

Nonna's Bolognese

This recipe is a family recipe passed on to me through my mother who got it from my grandma.
Her family is originally from Padova, Italy and she has been making bolognese like this her whole life.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Minced Meat
  • Tomatoes
  • Carrots
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti al dente
  2. Fry minced meat
  3. Fry tomatoes and carrrots
  4. Let it simmer for at least 1 hour
  5. Serve with fresh parmesan
