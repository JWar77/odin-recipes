Carbonara Recipe

Nonna's Carbonara

This recipe will help you make the most authentic carbonara right in your home kitchen
It will taste just like your imaginary "nonna" from Italy made it with all her heart and passion.

Ingredients as needed per Person:

  • 100 g Spaghetti
  • 1 Egg Yolk
  • 1 Full Egg
  • 70 g Pecorino
  • 50g Guanciale
  • Spaghetti Water
  • Ground Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti al dente
  2. While spaghetti are cooking start frying the guanciale
  3. Mix egg yolk, egg, pecorino and black pepper in a bowl
  4. Extract spaghetti from pot with fork
  5. Keep 50-100 ml of the spaghetti water
  6. Put spaghetti into pan with guanciale and turn off the heat
  7. Mix egg mixture, spaghetti and guanciale in pan without the egg lagging
  8. Serve on a plate
